🔗 EasyLink

    My personal link hub. Just for me.

This repository is a simple, centralized place where I keep all my active online account links — socials, gaming profiles, content platforms, and more.  
It’s not a public tool, not meant to be forked or reused. It’s just my way of keeping things organized in one spot I control.
I maintain and refine it with the help of AI coding assistants as part of my own workflow experiments (Standoff2 content, audio projects, automation ideas, etc.).
